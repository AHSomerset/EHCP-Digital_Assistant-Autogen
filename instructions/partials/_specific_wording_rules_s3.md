# Special Wording and Boilerplate Text Guide

**CRITICAL: This guide contains mandatory, verbatim text blocks for specific scenarios. When the conditions for a rule are met, the provided text must be used EXACTLY as written. These rules override all other style guides.**

---

### **Scenario 1: No Identified Health Needs**

**Condition:** When, after a comprehensive search, there are no identified **Health** Needs for the child (according to the Need Categorisation Guide).

**Action:** The Health Needs section MUST be populated with the following text verbatim.

*   **For the "Health Care Need 1" field, it must read:**
    > [NAME] has no identified needs for specialist health services at the current time.

*   **For the "Health Care Provision 1" field, it must read:**
    > [NAME] has no identified needs that require specialist health provision, however will continue to have access to universal services such as their GP and School nursing.

*   **For the "Health Care Outcome 1" field, it must read:**
    > Not applicable

All other Health Needs, Provisions, or Outcomes fields must be left blank.

---

### **Scenario 2: Awaiting Neurodiversity Assessment**

**Condition:** When the source documents indicate that the child is on a waiting list for, or is awaiting, a neurodiversity or neurodevelopmental assessment (e.g., for Autism or ADHD).

**Action:** The following text MUST be used verbatim.

*   **"Health Care Need 1" field, it must read:**
    > [NAME] has met the criteria to assess whether some of [his/her] difficulties can be explained by a neurodevelopmental diagnosis.

*   **For the "Health Care Provision 1" field, it must read:**
    > [NAME] is awaiting an assessment to see whether a neurodevelopmental condition may explain some of the difficulties [he/she] is experiencing. There is a support offer for [him/her], [his/her] parents, and education setting while [he/she] is waiting which can be accessed by calling the service advice line - Multi-disciplinary team, Children and Young Peopleâ€™s Neurodevelopmental Partnership, Somerset NHS Foundation Trust.

*   **The "Health Care Outcome 1" field must be left blank.**


### **Scenario 3: No Idenitified Needs (for any category other than health)

**Condition:** When, after a comprehensive search, there are no identified Needs for the child under any of the categories in the Need Categorisation Guide **(except for Health)**.

**Action:** The relavent Needs section MUST be populated with the following text verbatim.

*   **For the "Need 1" field, it must read:**
    > [NAME] has no identified has no identified special educational needs in this area.

*   **The relavent "Provision 1" field must be left blank.**

*   **The relavent "Outcome 1" field must be left blank.**
    
All other Needs, Provisions, or Outcomes fields in the relavent category must be left blank

---


### **Instructions for Placeholders:**

*   `[NAME]` must be replaced with the child's actual first name.
*   `[him/her]` must be substituted with the appropriate pronoun based on the child's gender; "him" for male, "her" for female.
*   `[his/her]` must be substituted with the appropriate pronoun based on the child's gender; "his" for male, "her" for female.
